My Story

I came to live at the Paw World shelter after someone cruelly threw boiling water on me, leaving painful burns. I couldn’t understand such cruelty.

Thanks to people like you, dogs like me have a place to heal. My recovery at the Paw World hospital was long, with surgeries and bed rest, but I always felt safe and cared for.

Now, five years later, I live at the sanctuary, always wearing a cheeky grin. I’m not fond of other dogs, but I adore humans—almost as much as my delicious meals!

While I wait for my forever home, you can become my sponsor and help with my ongoing care. I’ll send you updates and pictures to show how grateful I am!
